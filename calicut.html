KOZHIKODE

Kozhikode district in Kerala is one of the most important regions of the Malabar coast. Serene beaches, historic sites, wildlife sanctuaries, rivers and hills make Kozhikode a popular destination. With a legendary seaport, Kozhikode was previously the most crucial region of the Malabar coast.

IMPORTANT PLACES TO VISIT

  • Kozhikode Beach

  • Mannanchira-Square

  • Tali-Temple
